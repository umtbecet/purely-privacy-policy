Purely - Privacy Policy / Gizlilik Politikasi

Last updated / Son guncelleme: February 27, 2026

This policy explains how Purely handles data when you use the app. Bu politika, Purely uygulamasini kullanirken verilerinizin nasil islendigini aciklar.

1. Data We Collect / Toplanan Veriler

Purely app itself:

  • Scan history and favorites are stored locally on your device.
  • Purely uygulamasi tarama gecmisi ve favorileri cihazinizda yerel olarak saklar.

Third-party services used in app:

  • Google AdMob (ads) may collect advertising identifiers, device/app info, and usage data according to Google's policies.
  • Google Play Billing (in-app purchases/subscriptions) processes payment-related data via Google Play.

2. Camera Permission / Kamera Izni

Camera access is used only for barcode scanning functionality. Kamera erisimi sadece barkod tarama ozelligi icin kullanilir.

3. How We Use Data / Verileri Nasil Kullaniriz

  • To provide barcode scanning and product analysis.
  • To show ads (if enabled) and provide subscription features.
  • Uygulama ozelliklerini calistirmak, reklam gostermek ve abonelik ozelliklerini sunmak icin.

4. Data Sharing / Veri Paylasimi

Purely does not sell your personal data. However, data may be processed by third-party SDK providers (e.g., Google AdMob, Google Play) under their own privacy terms.

5. Data Retention and Deletion / Veri Saklama ve Silme

  • Local app data remains on your device until you clear app data or uninstall the app.
  • Cihazdaki yerel veriler uygulama kaldirilana veya uygulama verisi temizlenene kadar saklanir.

6. Children / Cocuklarin Gizliligi

The app is not specifically directed to children under 13. Uygulama ozellikle 13 yas alti cocuklara yonelik tasarlanmamistir.

7. Changes to This Policy / Politika Degisiklikleri

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page. Bu politika zaman zaman guncellenebilir; guncellemeler bu sayfada yayinlanir.
