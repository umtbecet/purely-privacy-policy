This Privacy Policy explains how Purely, published by UBCT ("we", "our", or "us"), handles data when you use the application.

1. Overview

Purely is designed to analyze products using barcode scanning and provide informational insights. We prioritize user privacy and do not collect personal identifiable information.

2. Data We Process

2.1 App Functionality Data

  • Scan history and favorites are stored locally on your device only.
  • Product-related data (such as ingredients, nutritional values, or similar information) may be processed to provide analysis results.
  • No personal data is stored on our servers.

2.2 Health-Related Information

The app may process product-related information such as nutritional values, ingredients, or similar data that can be considered health-related in order to provide analysis features.

  • This data is processed only to display information to the user.
  • No personal health records or medical data of the user are collected.
  • This data is not stored remotely and remains within the app context.
  • This data is not shared with third parties.

2.3 Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob: may collect advertising identifiers, IP address, device and usage data for ad personalization or analytics according to Google policies.
  • Google Play Billing: processes payments and subscription-related data securely.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No name, email, phone number, or personal identity information.
  • No personal health records or medical history.
  • No precise location data.

4. Permissions

Camera: Used only for barcode scanning. No images or videos are stored or transmitted.

5. How Data Is Used

  • To provide barcode scanning and product analysis.
  • To display nutritional and product insights.
  • To improve app performance and stability.
  • To display ads via AdMob.
  • To process payments via Google Play Billing.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share user data. Third-party services such as Google AdMob and Google Play may process limited data under their own privacy policies.

7. Data Storage

  • All app-generated data is stored locally on the user's device.
  • No backend or server storage is used by Purely.

8. Data Deletion

  • Users can delete all data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app.
  • No server-side deletion is required since no data is stored remotely.

9. Children’s Privacy

This application is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect data. However, no system is completely secure.

11. Changes

This policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be posted on this page.
