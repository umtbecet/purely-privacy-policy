Purely - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Purely, published by UBCT ("we", "our", or "us"), handles data when you use the application.

1. Overview

Purely is designed to analyze products using barcode scanning and provide informational insights. We prioritize user privacy and do not collect personally identifiable information.

2. Data We Process

2.1 App Functionality Data

  • Scan history and favorites are stored locally on your device only.
  • Product-related data, such as ingredients, nutritional values, or similar information, may be processed to provide analysis results.
  • No personal data is stored on our servers.

2.2 Health-Related Information

The app may process product-related information such as nutritional values, ingredients, or similar data that can be considered health-related in order to provide analysis features.

  • This data is processed only to display information to the user.
  • No personal health records or medical data of the user are collected.
  • This data is not stored remotely and remains within the app context.
  • This data is not shared with third parties.

2.3 Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob: may collect advertising identifiers, IP address, device information, and app usage data for advertising, analytics, or measurement purposes according to Google's policies.
  • Google Play Billing: processes payments, subscriptions, and related transaction data securely.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

  • No name, email address, phone number, or other direct personal identity information.
  • No personal health records or medical history.
  • No precise location data.

4. Permissions

Camera: Used only for barcode scanning functionality. No images or videos are stored or transmitted by Purely.

5. How Data Is Used

  • To provide barcode scanning and product analysis features.
  • To display nutritional and product-related insights.
  • To improve app performance, stability, and user experience.
  • To display advertisements via Google AdMob.
  • To process purchases and subscriptions via Google Play Billing.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. Certain limited data may be collected or processed by third-party services used in the app, such as Google AdMob and Google Play Billing, under their own privacy policies.

7. Data Storage

  • All app-generated data is stored locally on the user's device.
  • Purely does not use its own backend or remote server to store user data.

8. Data Deletion

  • Users can delete locally stored app data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app.
  • No server-side deletion request is needed because Purely does not store user data remotely.

9. Children's Privacy

This application is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data handled by the app. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with the revised date.
