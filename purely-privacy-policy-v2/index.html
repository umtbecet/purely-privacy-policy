Purely - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Purely, published by UBCT ("we", "our", or "us"), handles data when you use the application.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Data collected by the app

  • Scan history and favorites are stored locally on your device.
  • No personal data is stored on our servers by the app itself.

1.2 Data collected by third-party services

  • Google AdMob: may collect advertising identifiers, device information, IP address, and app usage data for personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • Google Play Billing: processes payments, subscriptions, and related transaction data.

2. Data We DO NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect or store personal identifiable information such as name, email, or phone number.
  • Purely does NOT collect, process, or analyze any health-related data or medical information.
  • We do NOT collect precise location data.

3. Camera Permission

Camera access is required only for barcode scanning functionality. No images or videos are stored or transmitted.

4. How We Use Data

  • To provide barcode scanning and product analysis functionality.
  • To improve app performance and user experience.
  • To display advertisements (via AdMob).
  • To process subscriptions and purchases (via Google Play).

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. However, certain data may be collected and processed by third-party services used in the app, such as Google AdMob and Google Play Billing, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

  • All app data (scan history, favorites) is stored locally on your device.
  • Data can be deleted by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.
  • No server-side data storage is performed by Purely.

7. User Rights

Users can request information or deletion regarding their data by contacting us.

8. Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
